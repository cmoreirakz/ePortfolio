Company Analysis


The Consolidated Recruiting Group (CRG) workforce is based in High Point, North Carolina, and specializes in professional search and staffing, as well as HR services and insurance. Their areas of focus are on accounting, finance IT professionals, and HR. They are a firm that has combined four search and staffing firms into one all with over 20 years’ experience, that are all based in the Southeast with revenues of 75 million they are one of the top HR companies in the east. The company’s values are many such as having the clients’ best interest in mind, by creating trust, and building relationships. Communication is also a core belief during all circumstances, to ensure clients’ needs are met. Relationships with employees are also a value by allowing ownership, and a voice to allow creativity to serve quality to their clients. Other strengths include their reviews on job search websites which state that they are excellent in helping clients with their recruitment, as well as stating that they are extremely professional, and will go to the next level to give quality service. Reviews from past workers on the job search websites also say that the salaries are competitive.
This company is very relevant to what I am looking for in terms of the culture, and type of control I want over my responsibilities in a job. I love front end development, and this position is exactly what I am looking for to gain experience, and the company is has everything I am looking for in terms of their values, flexibility, and I am interested in taking part of their development projects.
